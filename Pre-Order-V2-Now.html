What's New

Smaller, Smarter, More Scalable

V2 is a ground-up redesign built around the feedback we've gathered from our V1 testers all season. A few highlights:

 
 Move it between hives in seconds. The magnetic Heater Insert lifts out tool-free and carries to the next hive, with no brood boxes to remove and no disassembly.
 
 Scale your apiary affordably. One Heater Insert works across any number of hives. To add a hive, you just add a Bottom Box Frame instead of buying a whole new unit.
 
 Fully wireless. Wireless in-hive sensors, over-the-air firmware updates over Wi-Fi, and wireless data download, so no cables and no SD card swaps needed.
 
 Fits standard Langstroth hives. Available in both 8-frame and 10-frame sizes, selected at checkout.
