Field Results
TH-6: A Problem Hive, Turned Around

Every field season has a hive that keeps you up at night. This year it was TH-6. Coming into treatment, it carried one of the heaviest mite loads in the entire program, testing at 10.7 percent, well above the threshold where a colony is considered at serious risk.

We ran a single ThermaHive thermotherapy treatment. Afterward, the mite load dropped to 0.85 percent. To make sure the result was real and not a fluke of sampling, we confirmed it with two separate mite washes. Both came back in the same range.

That is a knockdown of more than 90 percent from one treatment, with no miticides, no chemical residue in the hive, and no resistance concerns. This is exactly the kind of outcome ThermaHive was built to deliver.

And it is not just TH-6. Our latest mite washes have every hive we sampled sitting below the 3 percent threshold, from one colony testing at a clean 0 percent to our two most stubborn hives of the season, now down to 2.93 and 1.92 percent after running high earlier in the year. A few colonies are close enough to the line that we already have proactive follow-up treatments planned, but seeing an entire apiary under threshold, kept there without a single chemical, is the strongest whole-program signal we have had yet.

Harvest
A Big Week: ~250 lbs of Honey Pulled

This past week we pulled honey from our ThermaHive colonies, and the harvest was a big one: roughly 250 pounds of honey across 101 frames. It is a great sign of strong, healthy colonies, and a pretty satisfying reward after a busy treatment season.

There is a quieter story in that harvest worth pointing out. Because ThermaHive is completely chemical-free, we did not have to time our mite treatments around the honey flow or worry about pulling supers before applying anything. No chemical residues, no label-mandated waiting periods, no question of whether a treatment might taint the honey. When the frames were ready, we simply pulled them. That freedom, treat when the bees need it and harvest when the honey is ready, is one of the everyday advantages of heat over chemicals that is easy to overlook.

The ThermaHive V2: Now available for pre-order

What is new in V2:

  • Move it between hives in seconds. The magnetic Heater Insert lifts out tool-free and carries to the next hive, with no brood boxes to remove and no disassembly. Drop the Blank Insert in and it doubles as a screened bottom board.
  • Scale your apiary affordably. One Heater Insert works across any number of hives. To add a hive, you just add a Bottom Treatment Frame instead of buying a whole new unit.
  • Fully wireless. Wireless in-hive sensors, over-the-air firmware updates over Wi-Fi, and wireless data download, so no cables and no SD card swaps, ever.
  • One controller, up to 50 sensors. Monitor temperature and humidity in every corner of every hive across your whole operation from a single controller.
  • No drilling required. The wireless SHT31 sensor drops into standard bee space between frames, with no half-inch hole, no plugs, and no woodware modifications. BroodMinder compatibility is included out of the box.

V2 keeps everything that makes ThermaHive work, from its self-regulating 500W PTC heater and sunlight-readable display to its glove-friendly controls and IP65 weatherproof enclosure. It runs from a standard 110V outlet, and can be paired with a compatible solar-battery setup for off-grid yards.
